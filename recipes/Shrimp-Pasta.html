Shrimp Alfredo Pasta

Description

This is a dish Featureing rich and delicious Permasian Cream sausue

Since Pasta is heavy in cream its recomendad to have simple sald in the side

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Cream cheese
  • Heavy cream
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta. Meanwhile, add the butter, cream cheese, heavy cream, chicken broth, and garlic to a skillet over medium heat. Cook it for 5 minutes. Stir in the parmesan cheese, and let it bubble for about a minute.
